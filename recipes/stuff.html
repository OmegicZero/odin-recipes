Stuffed Mushroom

What's the cousin of the stuffed potato? A stuffed mushroom. Enjoy this small savory explosion of flavor in a single bite. It's perfect for parties, restaurants, and your wallet; it will thank you.

Ingredients

  • Mushrooms
  • Oil
  • Cream cheese
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Clean the mushrooms, then remove and chop the stems
  2. Cook the garlic and mushroom stems in oil. Set aside to cool
  3. Stir in the remaining ingredients to make the filling
  4. Fill each mushroom cap with the filling
  5. Bake the stuffed mushrooms in the preheated oven
